Grammar focus

Using na and pa

Na often means “already” or marks a change of state. Pa often means “still” or “yet.”

Kumain na ako.I have already eaten.
Kumakain pa ako.I am still eating.
Hindi pa ako kumakain.I have not eaten yet.
